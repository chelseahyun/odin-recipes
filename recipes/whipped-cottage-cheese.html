Whipped Cottage Cheese Recipe

Description

This recipe is so versatile and summary! Yet, creamy and zesty, it is packed with more proteins and significantly less carbs than its sister recipe using ricotta.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of full fat cottage cheese, preferably Good Culture Double Cream
  • Zest and juice of half lemon
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • High-quality EVOO
  • A small bunch of fresh dill or thyme
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Place cottage cheese, lemon juice, garlic and herbs in a food processor.
  2. Pulse ingredients few times until garlic is disintegrated and content is mixed.
  3. Add 2 tbs of extra virgin olive oil and sea salt into the mixture.
  4. Pulse a few more time until the mixture reaches proper creaminess.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and mix in lemon zest.
  6. Serve with extra zest and chopped fresh herbs to top it on your favorite toast or summer salad!
