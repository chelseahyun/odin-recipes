Kimchi Avocado Toast Recipe

Description

These avocado toasts are ready to impress the taste buds of you and your friends!

Crispy crostinis toasted with earthy EVOO and herbs, tangy kimchi and creaminess of perfectly ripened avocado will beautifully pair with your favorite cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • Baguette (makes 10-12 crostinis)
  • 2-3 ripe Avocados
  • 1 cup of Kimchi
  • High quality EVOO
  • Herbs
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional: mild miso butter

Steps

  1. Toast your bread with herbs and extra virgin olive oil on the medium heat pan until golden brown.
  2. Gently mash 2-3 avocados, add salt and crack some pepper.
  3. Optional miso butter spread: combine 1 tbs of mild miso with melted butter and spread on the crostinis.
  4. Place mashed avocado then kimchi on top of the spread.
  5. Drizzle more EVOO and garnish with more herbs.
